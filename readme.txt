Get your product catalog listed on Pricerunner with this new official plugin from Pricerunner.

== Description ==

Pricerunner is a service that compares product prices from 5000+ webshops across Denmark, Sweden, France, Germany, Austria, and the UK.

Increase your sales and online visibility by feeding your product catalog to the Pricerunner comparison service in 5 easy steps.

You can find more information about Pricerunner here:

* http://www.pricerunner.dk/

* http://www.pricerunner.se/

* http://www.pricerunner.fr/

* http://www.pricerunner.de/

* http://www.pricerunner.at/

* http://www.pricerunner.co.uk/

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Search for 'Official Pricerunner' in the Plugins -> Add New section of your admin panel.
2. Click the install now button, then click the activate button.
3. Navigate to 'Pricerunner Feed' in the WordPress navigation bar.
4. Enter your contact information and hit the activate button.
5. Wait for Pricerunner to contact you.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why do I have to activate my plugin? =

You'll need to activate your plugin as a confirmation of your webshop's identity in order for Pricerunner to contact you. This way, we're able to send information about where to find your feed to Pricerunner so they'll know where to fetch your product data from.
